Over My Dead Body


President of USA, Donald Trump, was kidnapped by terrorists from Mexico. Your mission is to rescue him. You have to kill all terrorists!
Be careful! You have only one minute! After that time, kidnappers will behead our president. And please, don't hurt Donald.

Click to play

(W, A, S, D = Move, SPACE = Jump, MOUSE = Look around, MOUSE CLICK = shoot)
